3d Pose Estimation

showcasing 3d pose estimation model

I am hereby presenting what I was able to achieve with 3D pose estimation at ViFive. You can test with your own image/camera here - though the app might take some time to load as I deployed using free resources!

Be mindful to use clear image/video to test the model. As I removed all pre & post processing, the model might struggle to locate you. I attached some test videos from myself.
